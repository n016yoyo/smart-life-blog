Best SweetBeacon Alternative in 2026 — GiftWallet Review

Published March 2026 · 6 min read

SweetBeacon (스윗비콘) was the go-to app for managing Korean gift cards (기프티콘). Then it shut down. Millions of users were left without a way to track their gifticons — and a wave of them started searching for alternatives.

After testing every option available in 2026, GiftWallet is the clear winner. Here's the full breakdown.

Short answer: GiftWallet is the best SweetBeacon replacement. It has AI barcode scanning, expiry alerts, multi-language support, and works for all Korean gift cards including Kakao Gift, Naver Pay, and CU.

Why SweetBeacon Users Are Switching

SweetBeacon had a loyal user base because it did one thing well: it tracked your gifticons and told you before they expired. When it shut down, users flooded app review sections of every alternative with the same question: "Does this work like SweetBeacon used to?"

The answer, for most apps, was: not really. Most gift card apps in Korea are built around loyalty programs or digital wallets — not pure expiry tracking. GiftWallet is the exception.

What Makes GiftWallet Stand Out

1. AI Barcode Scanning

This is the killer feature. Instead of manually typing in gift card numbers, you just point your camera at the barcode. GiftWallet reads it instantly, identifies the brand, and automatically sets up an expiry reminder. It works with Kakao Gift barcodes, CU gifticons, GS25 vouchers, and most other Korean gift card formats.

2. Smart Expiry Alerts

You can set reminders at 30 days, 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before expiry. The default is 7 days — enough time to actually use the gift card before it disappears forever. This alone replaces the core functionality that SweetBeacon users miss most.

3. Multi-Language Support

SweetBeacon was Korean-only. GiftWallet works in English, Korean, Japanese, and Chinese. For expats living in Korea or anyone who doesn't read Korean, this is a huge deal — most Korean gift card apps don't bother with English at all.

4. Works Offline

Your gift cards are stored locally. You can show a barcode at the register even without an internet connection. Given how unreliable in-store WiFi can be, this matters.

The Migration from SweetBeacon

There's no direct import from SweetBeacon (since it's shut down, there's no export either). But the process is simple: scan each card with GiftWallet's camera. For most users with 5-20 active gifticons, this takes under 5 minutes.

Is GiftWallet Really Free?

Yes, fully free. No subscription, no card limit, no premium tier. LEO Company makes it available at no cost. The only thing you give up is nothing — it's just a straightforward utility app.

Verdict

If you're looking for a SweetBeacon replacement, GiftWallet is the closest match — and in several ways, it's better. The AI scanning is faster than manually entering codes, the multi-language support opens it up to non-Korean speakers, and the expiry alert system is exactly what SweetBeacon users are looking for.

It's free, it works offline, and it's available on the App Store right now.
