What Makes a Good Food Expiry Tracker?

There are dozens of food inventory apps. Most are too complicated to use consistently. The ones that actually work share a few traits:

  1. Fast to add items — if adding a grocery haul takes 20 minutes, you'll stop doing it
  2. Reliable notifications — the alert has to arrive before the food is already bad
  3. Barcode scanning — manual entry creates friction that kills daily use
  4. Clean, simple UI — feature-bloat is the enemy of habit formation

Best Food Expiry Tracker Apps 2026

1. Fridgi — Food Expiry Tracker (Best Overall)

Built specifically for tracking when food expires. Scan a barcode, set a date, done. You get push notifications before things expire, and the interface is fast enough to use at the fridge. Multi-language (English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese). Completely free — no paywall, no subscription.

2. Fridgely

More features than Fridgi — recipe suggestions, shopping lists, storage location tracking. But the add flow is slower and some features are behind a paywall. Better for highly organized households.

+ Recipe integration, shopping list, detailed categorization

3. OurGroceries

Primarily a shared shopping list app, but includes expiry date tracking. Good for families who want shared grocery visibility. Not primarily focused on expiry tracking.

+ Shared household lists, reliable sync

4. NoWaste

European app with solid food waste tracking and statistics. Tracks what you actually throw away, not just expiry dates. Good for people who want to measure their food waste over time.

+ Waste statistics, good barcode database
- Freemium model, UI less intuitive than Fridgi

How to Actually Reduce Food Waste (Beyond the App)

An app works best when combined with a few simple habits:

  • Scan items when you put them away, not when you remember to
  • Keep the app's alert window at 3-7 days before expiry (not 1 day — that's often too late)
  • Move soon-to-expire items to a dedicated "use soon" shelf in your fridge
  • Do a weekly fridge check — the app makes this take 2 minutes instead of 10
