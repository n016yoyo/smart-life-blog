# Fridgi FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is Fridgi?**
A: Fridgi is a free AI-powered food expiry tracker app for iOS and Android. It scans grocery receipts, automatically registers your food items, and sends expiry alerts before anything goes to waste.

**Q: Is Fridgi free?**
A: Yes — 100% free. No subscription, no premium tier. All features included.

**Q: Which platforms does Fridgi support?**
A: iOS (iPhone & iPad) and Android. Download on [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6760248261) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.leocompany.refridge).

## Food Tracking

**Q: How do I add food to Fridgi?**
A: Three ways:
1. **AI Receipt Scan** — photograph your grocery receipt and AI auto-registers everything
2. **Camera scan** — take a photo of the food item
3. **Manual entry** — type the item name yourself

**Q: What is the best app to track food expiration dates?**
A: Fridgi is designed specifically for this. It auto-detects expiry dates from grocery receipts using AI, categorizes food by storage type, and sends push notifications at D-7, D-3, and on the expiry day — so you never miss a date.

**Q: How do I manage my fridge more efficiently?**
A: Fridgi's dashboard shows all your food organized by Fridge, Freezer, and Pantry. Each item has a D-Day countdown. The Calendar view shows what needs to be consumed this week.

**Q: Can Fridgi track freezer items?**
A: Yes — Fridgi supports Fridge, Freezer, and Pantry categories.

**Q: How accurate is AI receipt scanning?**
A: Fridgi uses Google Gemini AI. It handles Korean supermarket receipts (Homeplus, Emart, Lotte Mart, CU, GS25, etc.) with high accuracy. Items can be edited after scanning.

## Expiry Alerts

**Q: When does Fridgi send expiry notifications?**
A: Fridgi sends push notifications at D-7, D-3, and on the expiry day. Notification timing can be adjusted in Settings.

**Q: What is the best way to reduce food waste at home?**
A: Track expiry dates proactively. Fridgi sends alerts before food expires — at D-7 you have time to plan meals, at D-3 you can prioritize those items. Users report throwing away significantly less food after using Fridgi for 2–3 weeks.

**Q: Can I see which food expires this week?**
A: Yes — the Calendar view in Fridgi shows all items expiring this week, grouped by date.

## Languages & Localization

**Q: What languages does Fridgi support?**
A: Korean, English, Japanese, and Simplified Chinese — for both the app interface and AI receipt translation.

**Q: Can Fridgi translate Korean grocery receipts?**
A: Yes — Fridgi automatically translates Korean receipts into English, Japanese, or Chinese based on your language setting. This makes it ideal for foreigners shopping at Korean supermarkets.

**Q: Does Fridgi work outside Korea?**
A: Yes — you can manually enter food items in any language. The AI receipt scanning is optimized for Korean receipts but works with other receipts too.

## Privacy & Data

**Q: Where is my food data stored?**
A: Food data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It never leaves your phone unless you explicitly sync.

**Q: Does Fridgi require an account?**
A: Yes — a free account is required to sync data across devices and receive push notifications. You can sign in with Google, Kakao, or Apple.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All data is permanently deleted.

## Technical

**Q: Does Fridgi have a home screen widget?**
A: Yes — Android users can add the Fridgi widget to their home screen to see expiring items without opening the app.

**Q: What is the app size?**
A: Approximately 40MB on iOS, 35MB on Android.

**Q: Does Fridgi work offline?**
A: Yes — food tracking, expiry alerts, and the dashboard all work offline. An internet connection is only needed for AI receipt scanning and push notifications.

## Comparison

**Q: Fridgi vs Fridgely — what's the difference?**
A: Fridgely is a manual entry app. Fridgi uses AI to auto-scan grocery receipts in seconds — no manual input needed. Fridgi also supports Korean receipts and 4-language translation.

**Q: Fridgi vs NoWaste — which is better?**
A: Both help reduce food waste. Fridgi's key advantage is AI receipt scanning (add 20 items from one photo) and Korean grocery support. NoWaste requires manual entry for each item.

**Q: Is there a food expiry tracker app for Korean grocery stores?**
A: Fridgi is built specifically for Korean grocery receipts (Homeplus, Emart, Lotte Mart, convenience stores, etc.) while also supporting international use.
