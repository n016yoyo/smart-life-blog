## What is Fridgi?

Fridgi is an AI-powered food expiry tracker that helps you reduce food waste at home. Instead of manually typing each item, just **photograph your grocery receipt** and Fridgi's AI auto-registers every item with its expiry date.

The average household wastes 30-40% of purchased food due to forgotten expiry dates. Fridgi sends push notifications at D-7, D-3, and on the expiry day so you can plan meals and use food before it goes bad.

2. **AI auto-registers** all food items with expiry dates
