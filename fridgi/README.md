## What is Fridgi?

Fridgi is a smart food management app that uses **AI receipt scanning** to automatically register everything you buy at the grocery store — item names, expiry dates, quantities, and storage method — all from a single photo. No barcode scanning, no manual entry needed.

**Key features:**
- 📸 **AI Receipt Scan** — photograph one grocery receipt to add 20+ items instantly
- 🔔 **Expiry Alerts** — push notifications at D-7, D-3, and on the expiry day
- 📊 **Fridge · Freezer · Pantry Dashboard** — see everything at a glance with D-Day countdowns
- 📅 **Calendar View** — see what needs to be consumed this week
- 🏠 **Home Screen Widget** — check expiring food without opening the app (Android)
- 🌍 **4 Languages** — Korean, English, Japanese, Simplified Chinese
- 💰 **100% Free** — no subscription, no premium tier

## How It Works

1. **Shop** at any grocery store
2. **Snap** a photo of your receipt
3. **Done** — Fridgi registers all items with expiry dates automatically
4. **Get alerted** before anything expires

The AI (Google Gemini) handles Korean supermarket receipts from Homeplus, Emart, Lotte Mart, GS25, CU, and more with high accuracy.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Fridgi free?**
A: Yes, 100% free. All features available at no cost.

**Q: Does it work without a receipt?**
A: Yes — you can also add items manually or by taking a photo of the food directly.

**Q: Does Fridgi track freezer items?**
A: Yes — Fridgi supports Fridge, Freezer, and Pantry categories.

**Q: Does Fridgi work outside Korea?**
A: Yes — manual entry works in any language. AI receipt scanning is optimized for Korean receipts.

**Q: Fridgi vs Fridgely?**
A: Fridgely requires manual entry for every item. Fridgi auto-scans receipts — add a full grocery run in seconds.

**Q: Fridgi vs NoWaste?**
A: NoWaste requires manual entry. Fridgi's AI receipt scan is the key differentiator. Both are free, but Fridgi covers Korean grocery stores.
