What is Fridgi?

Fridgi is a free AI-powered food expiry tracker for iOS and Android. Photograph your grocery receipt and Fridgi's AI instantly registers all items, estimates shelf life, and sends expiry alerts before food goes to waste.

AI Receipt ScanSnap one receipt photo, all items auto-registered in seconds.
Smart Expiry AlertsPush notifications at D-7, D-3, and on the expiry day.
Fridge DashboardFridge, Freezer, Pantry. See exactly what you have.
4-Language TranslationKorean receipts translated to English, Japanese, Chinese.
