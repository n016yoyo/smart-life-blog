What is GiftWallet?

GiftWallet is an AI-powered manager for Korean gift cards (gifticons / 기프티콘). One photo is all it takes -- GiftWallet's AI reads the barcode, amount, brand, and expiry date, then displays everything in English, Japanese, Chinese, or Korean.

AI Auto-RecognitionScan any Korean gifticon. Brand, amount, barcode, expiry extracted instantly.
Multilingual TranslationKorean gift cards auto-translated to English, Japanese, Chinese.
One-Tap BarcodeFull-screen barcode display for in-store use.
Expiry RemindersCalendar sync + push notifications before expiry.

Supported Brands

1,000+ brands — Korean chains and global brands operating in Korea:

  • Global brands: McDonald's, Burger King, KFC, Krispy Kreme, Subway, Shake Shack, Nike, Adidas, Costco
  • Coffee: Starbucks, Ediya, Mega Coffee, Twosome Place
  • Convenience: CU, GS25, 7-Eleven, Emart24
  • Bakery & Food: Paris Baguette, Tous les Jours, Dunkin', Lotteria
  • Retail & Shopping: Coupang, Lotte Department Store, Olive Young, SSG

Any brand that issues Korean gifticons is supported — if the barcode is there, GiftWallet reads it.
