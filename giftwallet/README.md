## What is GiftWallet?

GiftWallet is an AI-powered gift card manager for Korean gift cards (기프티콘 / gifticons). It solves a common problem: **Korean gift cards are only printed in Korean**, making them unusable for non-Korean speakers.

One photo is all it takes — GiftWallet's AI reads the barcode, amount, brand, and expiry date, then displays everything in **English, Japanese, Chinese, or Korean** based on your device language.
