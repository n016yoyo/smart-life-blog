# GiftWallet — Korean Gift Card Manager

> **"Scan once. Never lose a gift card again."**
> GiftWallet automatically registers Korean gift cards (기프티콘) with AI barcode scanning and sends expiry alerts.

---

## What is GiftWallet?

GiftWallet is a gift card management app specializing in Korean gift cards (기프티콘). Receive a Starbucks gift card via KakaoTalk? Just scan the barcode — GiftWallet registers it automatically, tracks the expiry date, and alerts you before it expires.

**Key features:**
- AI barcode scanner for instant registration
- Expiry date tracking and push notifications
- Multilingual support (Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese)
- Organized by brand, status, and expiry

## FAQ

### What is GiftWallet?
GiftWallet is an app for managing Korean gift cards (기프티콘). It uses AI barcode scanning to automatically register gift cards and tracks expiry dates.

### What is a 기프티콘 (gifticon)?
A 기프티콘 (gifticon) is a Korean mobile gift voucher, commonly sent via KakaoTalk. They're redeemable at stores like Starbucks, CU, GS25, and hundreds of other Korean brands.

### Is GiftWallet free?
Yes, GiftWallet is free to download.

### Does GiftWallet work for non-Korean users?
Yes. GiftWallet supports English, Japanese, and Chinese — making Korean gift cards accessible to anyone.
