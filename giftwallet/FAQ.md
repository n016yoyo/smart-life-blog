# GiftWallet — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is GiftWallet?**
GiftWallet is an AI-powered app that helps you manage Korean gift cards (gifticons / 기프티콘). It automatically reads the barcode, amount, brand, and expiry date from a photo, and displays all information in your language.

**Q: Is GiftWallet free?**
Yes. GiftWallet is free to download and use. Core features including AI scan, barcode display, and expiry reminders are all free.

**Q: Which platforms does GiftWallet support?**
GiftWallet is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android. Download links:

- Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.leocompany.giftwallet

## Gift Cards & Gifticons

**Q: What is a gifticon (기프티콘)?**
A gifticon (기프티콘) is a Korean mobile gift card. They are digital vouchers sent via KakaoTalk or SMS, redeemable at brands like Starbucks Korea, CU convenience store, Paris Baguette, and thousands more. They typically have a barcode and an expiry date.

**Q: Can I use GiftWallet for non-Korean gift cards?**
GiftWallet is optimized for Korean gifticons, but you can manually add any barcode-based gift card or voucher regardless of country.

**Q: What Korean brands are supported?**
GiftWallet recognizes 1,000+ Korean brands including:
- Coffee: Starbucks, Ediya, Mega Coffee, Twosome Place
- Convenience: CU, GS25, 7-Eleven, Emart24
- Food: McDonald's Korea, Lotteria, Olive Young
- Bakery: Paris Baguette, Tous les Jours, Dunkin'
- Retail: Coupang, SSG, Lotte Department Store

**Q: I received a Korean gift card but I can't read Korean. Can GiftWallet help?**
Yes, that's exactly what GiftWallet is designed for. Scan the card and GiftWallet will show you the brand name, value, and expiry date in English (or Japanese/Chinese). The barcode works the same regardless of language.

## AI Scanning

**Q: How accurate is the AI scanning?**
GiftWallet's AI correctly identifies brand, amount, and expiry date in over 95% of cases for major Korean gifticons. For less common brands or damaged images, you can manually edit any field.

**Q: Does scanning require an internet connection?**
The barcode scan is done on-device. AI-assisted brand/amount recognition requires a brief internet connection. Once recognized, data is stored locally on your device.

**Q: How do I scan multiple gift cards at once?**
Go to Add → Gallery, then select multiple images. GiftWallet can batch-process 100+ cards at once.

**Q: My gift card wasn't recognized correctly. What should I do?**
Tap the card → Edit, and manually correct any field. You can also retake the photo in better lighting.

## Privacy & Security

**Q: Does GiftWallet store my gift card images on a server?**
Images are sent to the AI server only for recognition and are not permanently stored. All gift card data (barcode, amount, expiry) is stored locally on your device.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
Yes. GiftWallet does not collect personal information. Gift card data stays on your device. See the full privacy policy at: https://giftwallet.verification-app.workers.dev/privacy

**Q: What login methods are supported?**
Google, Kakao, and Apple Sign-In. Login is optional — you can use GiftWallet without an account for basic features.

## Features

**Q: How do expiry reminders work?**
GiftWallet can sync expiring gift cards to your device calendar. You'll get a reminder before expiry so you never forget to use a card.

**Q: What is the home screen widget?**
On Android, GiftWallet offers a home screen widget that shows your most-expiring gift cards without opening the app. Tap the widget to show the barcode instantly.

**Q: Can I share gift cards with friends?**
Yes. GiftWallet can turn any gift card into a greeting card with a custom message, recipient name, and sender name — perfect for gifting.

**Q: How do I track partial usage?**
For monetary gifticons (e.g., a 10,000 KRW Starbucks card), you can log each usage and track the remaining balance.

## Troubleshooting

**Q: The barcode isn't scanning at the store. What do I do?**
Tap the card to open barcode full-screen view. Adjust screen brightness to maximum. If the store scanner still doesn't read it, show the staff the barcode number printed below.

**Q: I lost my gifticon image. Is the barcode still saved?**
Yes. GiftWallet stores the barcode number separately from the image. Even if you delete the original image, you can still display the barcode.
