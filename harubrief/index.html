What is HaruBrief?

HaruBrief is a free AI morning briefing app for iPhone. Every morning, it analyzes your calendar and tells you your day summary, whether you have time for lunch, whether you'll leave work on time, and your burnout risk level. Open HaruBrief and know your day in 10 seconds.

AI Daily BriefingYour schedule summarized in natural language every morning.
Lunch DetectorIdentifies whether you have a free window for lunch today.
Departure PredictorAlerts if you'll likely work late today.
Burnout Risk AlertFlags overloaded days and weeks before it's too late.

How It Works

Connect your iPhone calendar (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook). HaruBrief uses Google Vertex AI (Gemini) to analyze your schedule and generate a briefing. Your calendar data is used only for briefing generation and is never stored or shared.
