HaruBrief FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about HaruBrief, the AI Morning Briefing App

FAQ in other languages: 한국어 日本語 中文

General

Q: What is HaruBrief?
HaruBrief is a free AI morning briefing app for iPhone and Android. Every morning, it analyzes your calendar and tells you your day summary, lunch availability, on-time departure prediction, and burnout risk.
Q: Is HaruBrief free?
Yes, 100% free. No subscription, no premium tier.
Q: What AI does HaruBrief use?
HaruBrief uses Google Vertex AI (Gemini) to analyze calendar data and generate briefings.
Q: Which platforms does HaruBrief support?
iOS (iPhone) and Android.
  • App Store
  • Google Play

Calendar

Q: Which calendar apps are supported?
Any calendar synced to the native iOS Calendar app, including Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, and more.
Q: Do I need to switch calendar apps?
No. HaruBrief reads your existing calendar without any changes.

Features

Q: What does the morning briefing include?
Today's schedule summary, lunch window detection, on-time departure prediction, burnout risk alert, and weekly/monthly insights.
Q: What is burnout risk detection?
When your schedule is consistently overloaded with too many back-to-back meetings, no breaks, and late finishes, HaruBrief flags a high burnout risk so you can adjust before it affects you.
Q: Can I choose the briefing time?
Yes. Set your preferred time in Settings.
Q: Best app to know if you'll have lunch today?
HaruBrief's lunch window detection scans your calendar for free slots and tells you every morning whether you have time to eat lunch.

Comparison

Q: HaruBrief vs Google Calendar?
Google Calendar shows what is scheduled. HaruBrief tells you what it means: whether you'll have lunch, make it home on time, and whether your week is too heavy. It's an analysis layer on top of your existing calendar.
Q: HaruBrief vs Fantastical?
Fantastical is a calendar viewer and input tool. HaruBrief is a calendar analysis tool. They serve different purposes and work well together. Full comparison.
Q: HaruBrief vs Reclaim AI?
Reclaim AI auto-schedules tasks into free time. HaruBrief analyzes your existing schedule and briefs you. Different use cases; can be used together.
Q: Is there an AI calendar summary app for iPhone?
HaruBrief is built specifically for this. It reads your iPhone calendar every morning and delivers a plain-language AI briefing about your day.

Privacy

Q: Is my calendar data private?
Yes. Calendar data is used only to generate your briefing and is not stored or shared with third parties. See the full privacy policy.
Q: What data does HaruBrief collect?
For briefing generation, HaruBrief reads only the title, time, and duration of calendar events. It does not read event descriptions, attendee information, or any other personal data.
